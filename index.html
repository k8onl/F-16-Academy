Objectives of F-16


Aiming Academy


To develop and train players using the latest methods, supervised by coaching staff accredited by the Asian Football Confederation.


Academy Focus


On the educational front, we aim to broaden players' awareness and motivate them to excel academically, as this is a key factor in their success in a professional football career.


Shop Academy


Showcasing talents through media exposure to increase their chances of joining professional clubs.


Academy's quest


To make a significant impact on Saudi sports by discovering talents and channeling them to professional clubs.


Keen Academy


F-16 Academy is committed to instilling solid principles and fostering good morals, sportsmanship, and fair competition.


Community


F-16 Academy supports the local community through sports events, workshops, and outreach programs to promote football and youth involvement.
